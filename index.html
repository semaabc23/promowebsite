HARRY STYLES

singer. songwriter. actor.

Music

harry styles' music career hit off in 2020 when he was on xfactor and was placed into the band, one direction which became an overnight sensation. the band released five albums over the course of six years. afterwards, harry began his solo career and released his first album named 'harry styles'. he has since then released a second album, 'fine line'.


Filmography

harry styles' film career began after leaving one direction. he began filming for dunkirk, a christopher nolan film initally. then overtime began to be noticed more and more in the film industry and has received many roles after that. currently he is filming for don't worry darling. he is rumored to be being a part of the upcoming mcu film, eternals.


Additional Projects

harry styles has always been working on the side on numerous projects. his brand tpwk has been widely known to spread his positive message to treat everyone with respect. he's also known for his engagement with gucci as has served as a model for many of their campaigns. his fashion influence was also recognized when he co-hosted the met gala in 2019.
